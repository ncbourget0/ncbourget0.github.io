Wain Stop

Wain Stop is an internet art collective that I accidentally fell into while working at my job at Showcase Cinema two years ago. I had been a long time follower of their absurd animations and comics for years even before working with them, and an even bigger follower of the entire movement they were a part of. During one of my last days at Showcase, I really connected with one of the ushers working there. We got to talking and he told me he was one of the people in the art club and wrote their comic. I was blown away. He loved my screenprints and so we got to work together making some CMYK process shirts for Wain Stop. The first four photos are from when the shirts were on display at the Pop Gun gallery in Willamsburg, New York. The other more colorful mono-layer prints are for the halloween edition of the shirts.

Foiled

This is one of my favorite screenprints I have created. It exemplified the true nature of the CMYK inks themselves through the foil of the balloons. There is an added graphic element that plays into the black layer with the face and the spikes.

Tender is the Flesh

This is another screenprint on paper I had made to show off a graphic skill I was trying to crack for years. When I learned how to screenprint in 2020 I was very interested in using 3D modeling softwares to create graphics. Simply by looking at other screenprinted work, it was obvious if printing on white, the highlights of the object could be negated from a color fill and the shadows were another halftoned layer on the bottom of the object. This print exemplifies the hurdles I overcame simply by experimenting. Hopefully I will be able to do more 3D graphics in the future.

Memoir

This poster is very disturbing to some groups of people. It simplifes my struggle with sexuality, porn addiction and masturbation. It is a digital gicl√©e print showing scenes from gay porn, chicken feet, and newspapers. On top of this is a mono layer screenprint with the words "No Omelette, Im Just Breaking Eggs" which is a spin on needing to break a couple eggs to make an omelette, in relation to fertilizing a woman's egg to make a baby. Instead, it's gay porn and masturbation, so there's no omelette to be made, just breaking eggs. The font I used in this poster was created painstakingly by using a syringe to spit out the same egg yolk over and over again to create different letterforms. The print itself was pulled with an ink made from laundry detergent and my own semen, and was reactive for a short period under a blacklight.

Reversal on Earth Day 2023

Reversal on Earth Day is a book I created to be a companion of the Mattel Viewmaster. This was to prove I could make an ambitious and massive fully screenprinted book with a completely disjointed premise. After the book was completed, it illustrated my unique visual style more than anything I had created that year. It had also shown my drive to complete a large project during the span of a week and a half.

Lyme Disease Awareness

This shirt was created as my first revised and clean screenprints. It is meant to replicate a public service announcement graphic shirt that was common back in the 1980s with many car safety and anti-drug vintage shirts. This one is for Lyme disease because I love how ticks look.
