Data Chapel

This is a sculpture I created out of spray lizard enclosure foam. It houses a Raspberry Pi with a touchscreen running a script I wrote in python. The script allows users to draw on the touchscreen in an environment similar to the signature panels at a grocery store. It is implied on the screen that it wants you to confess something - anything. All confessions drawn on the screen are saved directly to the drive.

Kuru Restaurant

Kuru is a fictional restaurant I created the brand identity for during a class project. The brand focuses on an almost theatrical performance of cannibalism. Foods are cannibal themed and the house staff is very cold, methodical and unfeeling. It is meant to be a fun critique on the cold dead unfeelingness of high society restruants that have truly already tasted everything else on the menu.

Me and You and Everyone

Me and You and Everyone is a poster I had created with a focus on the overwhelming feeling of a massive data survey. 100 people at MassArt were asked 21 questions and a few personal profile questions. The data poster shown provides all of these questions in a visual assault format. Images are appropriated from online sources, abrasive colors are stretched to their capacity and 3d tribal marks can be seen throughout.

Fitness Challenge

Fitness Challenge is a feature I implemented to the Fitness/Fitness+ app from Apple as a hypothetical class assignment. I struggle a lot with getting active, drinking water or eating right and so I wanted to create a hypothetical app in Figma stressing the importance of holding yourself accountable. The app will allow you to randomly find a partner or matchmake with friends in order to motivate each other to complete an assignment based on self improvement. The challenge progress is inputted by the user, and when the challenge is complete a Mantra can be spoke into the app that can be replayed by other challenge goers for a competitive motivation.

Everyman

This stuffed creature is something I created for the same Me and You and Everyone data survey assignment. It contains all of the most important data points from the survey on its skin. It was intended to be the amalgamation of all 100 people and what that would look like. I had created a couple scary videos associated with it as well as a scary webpage. When you get scared while watching these, the project is set up so you want to hold the Everyman and feel the 100 people holding you back.
